Novelty is not Rarity

Under a tiny annotation budget, training-free novelty (small reference set) is the best proxy for the rarity you can't yet measure, and a two-pronged pre-flight covers where it fails. The causal test is a queue we control.

Where to look next

Where to sample is a value-of-information question: answerable, leakage-free, and genuinely separate from what to label. The honest negative: spatial coverage, not embedding novelty, wins here.
